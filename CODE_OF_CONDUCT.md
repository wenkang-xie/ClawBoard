# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want ClawBoard to be an open, welcoming, and respectful project.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent
- focus on the work, not the person
- welcome different levels of experience
- give actionable feedback

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- abusive or insulting language
- doxxing or sharing private information
- sustained disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this code of conduct.
